About the Project

PROBLEM: People travelling with family member will cost more money than solo or couples.

THE SOLUTION: Family travel package can provide better price and which is include flight, hotel and kids activities.

MY ROLE:UX designer (Individual Project)

User Personas

Problem Statement

Travelers struggle to find a place that every member in the family would like to go according to their preferences, especially the kids, they can’t satisfy them in the same time and within budget.

Value Proposition

My organization Tramigo is developing a traveller planning app to help travelers to plan their travel journey within the budget and to satisfy every traveler in a group trip to solve contradictions during the trip.

We’re better because we can plan a trip based on what the user needs.

We’re believable because of good feedback from every returning customer.

Final Thoughts / Conclusion

In summary, spend long time in this project, I’ve learned the whole process of how to design an app for users.
